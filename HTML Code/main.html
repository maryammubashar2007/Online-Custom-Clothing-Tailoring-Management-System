Inside the House

A workroom of quiet hands and loud ambition.

Velvara is not a brand assembled in a season. It is a workroom — a sun-lit floor of cutters, embroiderers and pattern-masters who have, in many cases, been with the house for over twenty years.

  • i.
    Commissioning

    A private 90-minute consultation to understand the occasion, the silhouette, and the story you want stitched into it.

  • ii.
    Sampling & Embroidery

    Muslin toiles, fabric sourcing, and motif development — typically 6 to 14 weeks of handwork.

  • iii.
    Three Fittings & Final Reveal

    Two intermediate fittings and a ceremonial reveal at the atelier, presented in our heritage trousseau case.
